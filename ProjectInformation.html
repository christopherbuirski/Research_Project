Lebenya Farm and the Stonewall Site

Lebenya Farm is located near the town of Swartruggens which is in the Zeerust-Pilanesberg-Rustenburg (ZPR) region. The ZPR region includes the Groot Marico River to the West, the Magaliesberg range to the Southeast and the Pilanesberg volcanic complex to the North.
